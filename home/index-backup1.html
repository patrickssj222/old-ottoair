About OttoAir



What is OttoAir?

OttoAir provides the cheapest flight ticket between Canada and China. Airplane tickets are often bundled with unnecessary services to inflate ticket price. OttoAir dedicates ourselves to remove such costs from undesired services so travelers only pay for what’s truly important.

How Do We Do It?

A major cost hiding in plane tickets is luggage transportation. Due to the high demand from large e-commerce organizations (such as Amazon and Walmart) for fast international shipping, OttoAir is able to provide a platform for them to send gifts as Ottopackages via our travellers. In turn, OttoAir can help reimburse our travellers’ unused luggage space with greatly reduced traveling expenses.

What is OttoPackage?

OttoPackages only contains gifts from popular e-commerce sites. These packages are sent from UPS, FedEx, DHL, Canada Post, and other TSA certified shipping companies. As a traveler with us, you will save up to 50% of your ticket price by carrying OttoPackages. Read more about our detailed service procedure here.
