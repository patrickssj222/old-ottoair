About Us

In OttoAir, we are not marketing our product nor the service, we are promoting a new life style. We believe traveling internationally should be easy, inexpensive, and instantaneous. You should be able to hop on a plane after you have read a fantastic blog and just suddenly decide to see it in person. China is not about the Forbidden City, the Great Wall, nor the world’s biggest variety of flavors, it’s about the culture, and the interaction of people. OttoAir focus on optimize your traveling experience, by providing you the cheapest ticket, and the insightful guidance.

Detailed Procedure

  1. Pick your flight date, and register your account.
  2. Upload your passport, the page that has your name and document number. If the picture is blurry, OttoAir’s customer service will reach out to you.
  3. Select the numbers of OttoPackage you want to travel with. The more you choose, the more you save.
  4. Pay your flight fare through credit cards, debit cards or PayPal.
  5. Check for your confirmation email, and make sure your info is correct. Before your departure, OttoAir’s courier will come to your door to load items into your own suitcase, you can inspect all items if you wish. (All of our items are gifts from certified E-commerce site. They are sent and inspected by FedEx, Ups, DHL, Canada Post, and other TSA certified shipping companies.)
  6. You need to sign an agreement indicates you must not open the OttoPackage after the loading process.
  7. Bring your OttoPackage through security check.
  8. Have a nice sleep
  9. After you have arrived at the local airport, take your OttoPackage home.
  10. OttoAir’s customer service will assist you with the drop off process, the arrangement will be made 2 days within your arrival. The local shipping company will come to your door to take the OttoPackage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to use my own suitcases for OttoPackage?

Yes, we need your suitcases. (the required size of suitcase: height 67cm- 76 width 45cm-48, submitting an unqualified suitcase will cause ticket price variations) Our courier will contact you and come to your door to retrieve your suitcases.

Does OttoAir provide airport transfer service?

Yes, if you purchase ticket from OttoAir, you can order a transfer service for additional $25.

Do I know what’s inside the OttoPackage?

Yes, a list of all items will send to you through email.

Can I inspect or open the OttoPackage myself?

You are welcome to inspect your OttoPackage when it is delivered to you. The OttoPackage will be sealed after your inspection, and you will sign an agreement indicates the OttoPackage will not be opened by you.

What would happen if I don’t find the post office at airport?

If you are unlucky enough, the post office is not available, you need to take the luggage home. OttoAir’s customer service will assist you to download the APP of the local shipping company, and arrange their team to pick up the OttoPackage at your door
